Privacy Policy

Last Updated: August 14, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to SmartPark. Your privacy is important to us, and this policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information while using our parking management system.

2. Information We Collect

  • Personal details such as name, phone number, and email address when registering.
  • Vehicle information including license plate number for slot allocation and verification.
  • Payment details for processing automated transactions via UPI or wallet.
  • Location data for identifying nearby parking slots (if enabled by the user).
  • Usage logs for monitoring and improving our services.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To allocate and manage parking slots in real time.
  • To process secure payments for parking services.
  • To enhance safety through monitoring and verification features.
  • To send booking confirmations, reminders, and updates.
  • To improve user experience by analyzing service usage patterns.

4. Data Protection

We implement strict security measures to protect your information, including encryption, secure payment gateways, and restricted access to sensitive data.

5. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share necessary data only with:

  • Authorized staff for verification and slot management.
  • Payment processors for completing transactions.
  • Legal authorities if required by law.

6. Your Rights

  • Access and review your personal data.
  • Request corrections to your information.
  • Request deletion of your account and data.
  • Opt-out of promotional communications.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with the updated date.
